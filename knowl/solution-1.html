Solution 1.2.1.1.

Remove 4 V source and find \(I_{1}\) in the circuit.
\begin{equation*} I_{1b} = \frac{V}{R_{eq}} = \frac{2}{[2+6||4]} =\frac{10}{22} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \therefore\quad I_{1} = I_{1b}\times\frac{6}{6+4} = \frac{6}{22} \,A \end{equation*}
Remove 2 V source and find \(I_{2}\) in the circuit.
\begin{equation*} I_{2b} = \frac{V}{R_{eq}} = \frac{4}{[6+2||4]} =\frac{12}{22} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \therefore\quad I_{2} = I_{2b}\times\frac{2}{2+4} = \frac{4}{22} \,A \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \therefore\quad I = I_{1}+I_{2} = \frac{6}{22}+\frac{4}{22} = \frac{10}{22} \,A \end{equation*}
